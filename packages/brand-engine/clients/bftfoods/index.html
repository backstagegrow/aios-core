Our Services

End-to-end expertise,
not just logistics

From market intelligence to financial solutions, we handle the full complexity of international food trade so you can focus on growth.

Export Consulting
For companies with little or no international sales experience, BFT diagnoses and manages the entire export process — from identifying market opportunities to closing deals and increasing market share.
Trading
We buy and sell animal proteins and agricultural commodities, working collaboratively with suppliers to promote products competitively and intelligently in global markets.
Specialized Logistics
Our extensive experience and carrier relationships allow us to negotiate special rates with shipping companies worldwide — delivering competitive pricing and reliable cold-chain solutions.
Financial Services
Immediate payment to suppliers after container loading. Extended payment terms for importers so they can focus on selling. We remove cash flow as a barrier to trade.
Quality Control
Our dedicated QC team regularly visits producer partners to validate compliance, food safety standards, and certification requirements before any shipment is approved.
Market Intelligence
Asset optimization, risk management, and market access — we translate market data into actionable strategies for buyers and sellers navigating specialized food markets.
